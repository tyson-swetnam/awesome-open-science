AAAS List - meta list of funding sources

List of Funding Agencies - other than US NSF

Global¶

UNESCO - Recommendation on Open Science provides an international framework for open science policy and practice that recognizes disciplinary and regional differences in open science perspectives

Americas¶

Canada¶

Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (NSERC) - Canada's federal research funding agency

Mexico¶

CONAHCYT (formerly CONACYT) - Mexico's science and technology council, elevated to Secretariat level in 2025

UC-MEXUS - University of California Institute for Mexico-US collaborative research

Ciencia abierta - open science policy in Latin America

Central America¶

CSUCA - Central American Higher Education Council coordinating regional research

AACR MONARCA Grant - cancer research grants for Latin America researchers

United States¶

Science.gov - Open Science Announcements from Federal Agencies

The Navigation Fund Open Science Grants - funding for open science initiatives and tools

Early Career Funding Opportunities - Johns Hopkins curated list for early career

Grant Explorer - search funded projects from NSF, NIH, DoD

National Science Foundation (NSF) - US federal agency funding fundamental research

National Institutes of Health (NIH) - largest public funder of biomedical research

Department of Energy (DoE) - funds physical sciences and energy research

National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) - NASA research funding opportunities through ROSES

National Institute of Food and Agriculture (NIFA)

National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) - funding for ocean, coastal, and atmospheric research

South America¶

Argentina:

CONICET - Argentina's National Scientific and Technical Research Council, ranked #1 in Latin America

Brazil:

CNPq - National Council for Scientific and Technological Development

CAPES - Brazilian federal agency for graduate education

Chile:

ANID - National Research and Development Agency (formerly CONICYT)

Fondecyt - Chile's main basic research funding program

Colombia:

Minciencias - Ministry of Science, Technology and Innovation (formerly COLCIENCIAS)

Peru:

CONCYTEC - National Council of Science, Technology and Innovation

Regional Latin America¶

RedCLARA - Latin American research and education network

LA Referencia - Latin American network of institutional repositories

SciELO - Scientific Electronic Library Online for Latin America

AmeliCA - open knowledge cooperative infrastructure for Latin America

Oceania¶

Australian Academy of Science - Australian open science policy and resources

New Zealand MBIE - NZ Ministry funding science and innovation

Europe¶

European Research Council (ERC) - funds investigator-driven frontier research across all fields through Starting, Consolidator, Advanced, and Synergy Grants

UK Grants Online - searchable database of UK funding opportunities

UK Research and Innovation (UKRI) - umbrella organization for UK research councils

Horizon Europe - an ambitious 85 billion euros research and innovation programme that started 2021 and will run for seven years

European Commission - Information about research and innovation funding programmes, including Horizon Europe, the Cohesion Fund, LIFE, ERDF, ESIF and RFCS

German Research Foundation (DFG) - Germany's central research funding organization supporting independent research

Agence Nationale de la Recherche (ANR) - French national research funding agency

Netherlands Organisation for Scientific Research (NWO) - Dutch research council funding scientific research

Swiss National Science Foundation (SNSF) - Switzerland's premier research funding organization

Open Research Area (ORA) - collaboration between ANR (France), DFG (Germany), ESRC (UK) and NWO (Netherlands) for social sciences research

Asia-Pacific¶

Japan¶

Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS) - Japan's premier research funding agency supporting international collaboration and researcher mobility

e-ASIA Joint Research Program - multilateral research funding program among East Asian countries

China¶

National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) - China's primary research funding agency for basic research

India¶

Indian Council of Social Science Research (ICSSR) - India's premier social science research funding organization

Department of Science and Technology (DST) India - Indian government agency for science and technology research

Singapore¶

National Research Foundation Singapore - sets research and development direction and funds strategic research initiatives

South Korea¶

National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF) - Korea's primary research funding agency

Africa¶

Continental Organizations¶

African Academy of Sciences (AAS) - leading African science academy with multiple funding programs

AESA (Alliance for Accelerating Excellence in Science in Africa) - programmatic arm of AAS mobilizing R&D funding

TWAS (World Academy of Sciences) - research grants for developing countries in Africa

TWAS Seed Grant for New African PIs - grants for early-career African scientists under 40

Science Granting Councils Initiative (SGCI) - strengthening science funding agencies in Sub-Saharan Africa

Science4Africa - resources for African researchers

National Agencies¶

Kenya:

National Research Fund (NRF Kenya) - Kenya's national research funding agency

South Africa:

National Research Foundation (NRF South Africa) - South Africa's leading research funding agency

Foundations¶

Science Philanthropy Alliance - coalition of philanthropies supporting basic scientific research

Alfred P. Sloan Foundation - supports research in science, technology, engineering, mathematics, and economics

Arnold Ventures - evidence-based philanthropy with open science guidelines for research grants

Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation - global health and development research with open access policy

Chan Zuckerberg Initiative (CZI) - supports open science including Essential Open Source Software for Science program (partnership with Kavli and Wellcome)

Gordon and Betty Moore Foundation - funds scientific discovery and environmental conservation research

Kavli Foundation - supports fundamental research in astrophysics, nanoscience, neuroscience, and theoretical physics

Wellcome Trust - UK-based global health research foundation supporting open science and open source software (partner in CZI-Kavli-Wellcome Essential Open Source Software initiative)

Open Research Funders Group (ORFG) - consortium of funding organizations committed to open research practices

John D. and Catherine T. MacArthur Foundation - supports creative people, effective institutions, and influential networks

OceanKind - supports ocean conservation and marine science research

Research Corporation for Science Advancement - private foundation advancing fundamental research in physics, chemistry, and astronomy

Robert Wood Johnson Foundation - largest philanthropy focused on health in the United States

Simons Foundation - supports research in mathematics and the basic sciences

Templeton World Charity Foundation - funds research exploring big questions in science, philosophy, and theology
