DMP Tools & Templates¶

DMP Tool - free, community-supported service by UC Curation Center that creates machine-actionable DMPs meeting funder requirements and open science best practices. Includes 1000+ public sample plans.

Data Stewardship Wizard - publicly available web-based tool developed within ELIXIR infrastructure providing FAIR metrics and guiding users to make data as FAIR as possible

DMPonline - web-based tool by Digital Curation Centre (DCC) with pre-set templates based on specific funder policy documents

Argos OpenAIRE - open source DMP tool developed within OpenAIRE and EUDAT projects, linked to ORCID, OpenAIRE services, and Zenodo repository

Research Data Management Organiser (RDMO) - assemble all relevant planning information and data management tasks across the whole life cycle of research data

OSF DMP Creation Guide - guidance for creating DMPs within Open Science Framework
