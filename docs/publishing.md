## Pre-Print Servers

### Multidisciplinary

[ASAPbio Pre-Print Server List](https://asapbio.org/preprint-servers){target=_blank} - comprehensive directory of preprint servers maintained by ASAPbio, a scientist-driven non-profit promoting transparency in life science communication.

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/){target=_blank} - free distribution service and open-access archive for 2.4+ million scholarly articles in physics, mathematics, computer science, quantitative biology, quantitative finance, statistics, electrical engineering and systems science, and economics.

[OSF.io Preprints](https://osf.io/preprints/){target=_blank} - multidisciplinary preprint platform operated by Center for Open Science with support for multiple domains.

[Preprints.org](https://www.preprints.org/){target=_blank} - free multidisciplinary preprint server supported by MDPI covering all scientific disciplines.

[Research Square](https://www.researchsquare.com/){target=_blank} - preprint platform integrated with major publishers for rapid sharing before peer review.

### Life & Health Sciences

[BioRxiv](https://www.biorxiv.org/){target=_blank} - open access preprint repository for biological sciences, now operated by openRxiv (launched 2025).

[medRxiv](https://www.medrxiv.org/){target=_blank} - preprint server for health sciences, now operated by openRxiv alongside bioRxiv.

[EcoEvoRxiv](https://www.ecoevorxiv.com/){target=_blank} - preprint server for ecology, evolution, and conservation science.

### Physical Sciences & Engineering

[ChemRxiv](https://chemrxiv.org/){target=_blank} - preprint server for chemistry, chemical engineering, materials science, and related disciplines.

[engrXiv](https://engrxiv.org/){target=_blank} - open preprint server for engineering covering chemical, civil, environmental, materials, mechanical, and other engineering fields.

[TechRxiv](https://www.techrxiv.org/){target=_blank} - IEEE-affiliated preprint server for electrical engineering, computer science, and technology research.

### Earth & Space Sciences

[ESSOar](https://www.essoar.org/){target=_blank} - Earth and Space Science Open Archive hosted by the American Geophysical Union.

### Social Sciences & Humanities

[SocArXiv](https://osf.io/preprints/socarxiv){target=_blank} - open archive for social science research across all disciplines.

[MetaArXiv](https://osf.io/preprints/metaarxiv){target=_blank} - preprint server focused on research transparency, reproducibility, and meta-science.

### Regional Preprint Servers

[AfricArXiv](https://info.africarxiv.org/){target=_blank} - pan-African open access preprint repository for research about Africa or by African scientists, established 2018.

[IndiaRxiv](https://indiarxiv.org/){target=_blank} - India's first preprint server for domestic research across all disciplines, relaunched 2024.

## Financial Support for Open Access Publishing Fees

### National & Regional Programs

[HINARI](http://www.emro.who.int/information-resources/hinari/){target=_blank} - WHO program providing free or low-cost journal access to low- and middle-income countries.

[Max Planck Digital Library](https://group.springernature.com/gb/group/media/press-releases/landmark-agreement-between-springer-nature-and-mpdl/18498782){target=_blank} - transformative agreement enabling German authors to publish open access in Springer Nature journals without APC fees.

[Bibsam Consortium](https://www.springernature.com/gp/open-research/institutional-agreements/oaforsweden){target=_blank} - Swedish consortium agreement covering open access fees in Springer Nature research journals.

[PALCI ACS Agreement](https://www.psu.edu/news/university-libraries/story/scientific-publisher-agreements-expand-open-access-research){target=_blank} - Partnership for Academic Library Collaboration & Innovation agreement eliminating author publishing charges for ACS journals (through 2026+).

### University Library OA Funds

[MIT Libraries OA Publishing Support](https://libraries.mit.edu/scholarly/publishing/oa-publishing-support/){target=_blank} - MIT agreements with PLOS, Taylor & Francis, and other publishers providing no-cost OA publishing for MIT authors through 2026.

[Open Access Publishing Fund - Florida State](https://www.lib.fsu.edu/research-and-publish/publishing/open-access-funding){target=_blank} - financial support for Article Processing Charges at FSU.

[Indiana University Open Access Fund](https://research.iu.edu/funding-proposals/funding/opportunities/open-access-journal-fund/index.html){target=_blank} - IU program supporting APC costs for fully open access journals.

[Tufts Tisch Library OA Fund](https://tischlibrary.tufts.edu/about-library/library-awards/tisch-library-open-access-publishing-fund){target=_blank} - up to $30,000 available for 2025-2026 academic year for open access publishing.

### International Funding

[Horizon Europe Open Research Europe](https://open-research-europe.ec.europa.eu/){target=_blank} - European Commission's no-cost open access publishing service for Horizon Europe grant beneficiaries.

[SciDev.Net](https://www.scidev.net/global/){target=_blank} - science and development news network supporting open access in developing countries.

## Open Access Publishing

### Open Access Directories & Indexes

[Directory of Open Access Journals (DOAJ)](https://doaj.org/){target=_blank} - curated directory of 22,464+ peer-reviewed open access journals from 140 countries with 12+ million article records; 14,024 journals charge no fees.

[SciELO](https://scielo.org/){target=_blank} - Scientific Electronic Library Online covering 16 countries across Latin America, Caribbean, South Africa, Spain, and Portugal; pioneering regional diamond open access since 1997.

[Redalyc](https://www.redalyc.org/){target=_blank} - Red de Revistas Científicas de América Latina y el Caribe indexing 1,500+ diamond open access journals with 750,000+ articles from 25 countries.

### Diamond Open Access Publishers

Diamond (or platinum) open access means no fees for readers OR authors - 100% free publishing and access.

[MIT Press Direct to Open (D2O)](https://mitpress.mit.edu/mit-press-expands-direct-to-open-d2o-open-access-model-in-2026-with-publishing-partners/){target=_blank} - diamond open access model for scholarly monographs, expanding in 2026 with Duke University Press and Goldsmiths Press.

[Beilstein Journals](https://www.beilstein-journals.org/){target=_blank} - diamond open access journals in chemistry and nanotechnology, fully funded by the Beilstein-Institut foundation in Germany.

[Diamond Scientific Publishing](https://www.diamondopen.com/){target=_blank} - international publisher of diamond open access books, journals, and conference proceedings across multiple fields.

[Emerald Diamond OA Journals](https://www.emeraldgrouppublishing.com/publish-with-us/open-research-emerald/diamond-open-access-journals){target=_blank} - diamond open access journals published in partnership with universities and associations.

[Cambridge Diamond](https://diamond-oa.lib.cam.ac.uk/){target=_blank} - University of Cambridge's platform supporting diamond open access publishing initiatives.

### Major Open Access Publishers

[PLOS (Public Library of Science)](https://plos.org/){target=_blank} - pioneering nonprofit open access publisher founded 2000, publishing seven peer-reviewed journals across all sciences.

[MDPI](https://www.mdpi.com/){target=_blank} - Swiss publisher of 400+ open access journals across sciences, technology, and medicine; many institutions offer 10% APC discounts.

[Frontiers](https://www.frontiersin.org/){target=_blank} - open access publisher with journals across sciences, technology, medicine, and humanities using collaborative peer review.

[F1000Research](https://f1000research.com/){target=_blank} - innovative open access platform with rapid publication, open peer review, and support for data sharing across biomedical, life, and physical sciences.

[PeerJ](https://peerj.com/){target=_blank} - open access publisher of seven journals in biomedical, life, environmental, physical, and computer sciences with affordable APCs.

[eLife](https://elifesciences.org/){target=_blank} - nonprofit open access journal for biomedical and life sciences, known for innovative peer review and the Sciety preprint review platform.

[Copernicus Publications](https://publications.copernicus.org/){target=_blank} - largest open access publisher in geo- and Earth system sciences since 2001, pioneering interactive open peer review.

### Traditional Publishers with OA Options

[AAAS Science Open Access](https://www.science.org/content/page/open-access-aaas){target=_blank} - American Association for Advancement of Science open access publishing in Science family journals.

[Nature Portfolio Open Access](https://www.nature.com/nature-portfolio/open-access){target=_blank} - Springer Nature's 600+ open access journals plus OA options in hybrid journals.

[American Geophysical Union (AGU)](https://www.agu.org/Publish-with-AGU/Publish/Open-Access){target=_blank} - Earth and space sciences open access publishing across 20+ journals.

[CSIRO Publishing](https://www.publish.csiro.au/){target=_blank} - Australian research organization's open access options across environmental, agricultural, and physical sciences.
