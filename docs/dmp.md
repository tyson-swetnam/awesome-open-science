## :octicons-repo-template-24: DMP Tools & Templates

[:octicons-stack-24: DMP Tool](https://dmptool.org/){target=_blank} - free, community-supported service by UC Curation Center that creates machine-actionable DMPs meeting funder requirements and open science best practices. Includes 1000+ public sample plans.

[Data Stewardship Wizard](https://ds-wizard.org/){target=_blank} - publicly available web-based tool developed within ELIXIR infrastructure providing FAIR metrics and guiding users to make data as FAIR as possible

[DMPonline](https://dmponline.dcc.ac.uk/){target=_blank} - web-based tool by Digital Curation Centre (DCC) with pre-set templates based on specific funder policy documents

[Argos OpenAIRE](https://argos.openaire.eu/){target=_blank} - open source DMP tool developed within OpenAIRE and EUDAT projects, linked to ORCID, OpenAIRE services, and Zenodo repository

[Research Data Management Organiser (RDMO)](https://rdmorganiser.github.io/){target=_blank} - assemble all relevant planning information and data management tasks across the whole life cycle of research data

[OSF DMP Creation Guide](https://help.osf.io/article/144-creating-a-data-management-plan-dmp-document){target=_blank} - guidance for creating DMPs within Open Science Framework
